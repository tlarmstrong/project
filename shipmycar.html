The Contracting Process

How it works - who's involved and what does a Broker do?

Who is involved:

  1. The Car Owner wanting to ship a personal vehicle – That’s you!

  2. The Carrier – The trucking company that picks up, transports and delivers the vehicle.

  3. The Broker – The expert in matching the needs of people shipping cars and Carriers.

What the Broker does:

  • Searches for a reputable and reliable Carrier.
  • Contracts with the Carrier and is responsible for managing the Carrier relationship and reviewing and agreeing to the Carrier’s Terms and Conditions governing the transport.
  • Required by the Federal government to carry a bond securing the payments among the parties to the contract, providing you a layer of legal and financial protection.
  • Responsible for handling any difficulty that might arise with the transport.
