Our Mobile Solution

Mobile Tools

Import vehicle photos, scan a VIN, enable GPS tracking, provide status updates, and e-sign documents with iOS (iPhone®) or Android® mobile devices.

Mobile Theme

In our modern world we need online tools to work with mobile devices. We offer an easy-to-use mobile theme with the perfect mix of tools.

Top Features

Carrier Profile

A carrier profile can include company name, customer rating, driver name and photo, trailer detail, trailer photo and more.

VIN Scan

Auto Load Logic is driven by Vehicle Identification Numbers (VIN) to eliminate duplication and errors commonly found on other platforms.

Integrated Communications

Registered users can communicate with all parties involved with each shipment through the Auto Load Logic internal Private Messaging system.

Vehicle Tracking

Our real-time GPS Tracking feature allows brokers, shippers, carriers and customers to check location and status online anytime.

Photo Inspection

E-Bills of Lading and Photo Inspections from mobile devices help provide greater levels of assurance and confidence to all parties involved in each shipment.

e-Signatures

With e-Signatures at both pick-up and delivery, the shipper and broker each will know immediately when the vehicle is loaded and delivered.
